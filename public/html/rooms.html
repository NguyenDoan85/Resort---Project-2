Our Rooms and Bungalows

Beach Bungalow

We have 3 Beach Bungalow, each Beach Bungalow has a large double bed or two single beds, with a room area of 387.5 square feet. The room is facing the sea, convenient for traveling between swimming pool and sea.

Grande bungalow room is designed in ancient Vietnamese architectural style, has a balcony with a wide garden view. There is a room area of 484.4 square feet, the room includes two large beds and large space suitable for small families.

Pool Bungalow

The room is located near the swimming pool, with a room area of 344.4 square feet. Room includes two large / small beds.

Superior Bungalow

Superior Bungalow has balcony with garden view, with room area of 323 square feet. Rooms are arranged in a long row on the resort grounds. There are two types of Superior Bungalow: the room type includes two small / large single beds and the room type includes a double bed.

Deluxe room located on a building with balcony overlooking the sea, with an area of 430.6 square feet. The room is divided into each floor: ground floor, first floor and second floor. The room includes two large beds.

Superior Room

Superior room is located on a building including ground floor, first floor and second floor, garden view room with room area of 344.4 square feet. The room is decorated in a classic style and has a large glass door frame that overlooks the surrounding area. Room includes two single beds.
